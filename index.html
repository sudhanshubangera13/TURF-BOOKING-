ABOUT THE COMPANY

Our city, Nitte, has been missing an all-inclusive sports and entertainment facility. To address these shortcomings, we decided to start Cross Fit Arena in January 2021. It's a new experience that seeks to balance competition and entertainment while promoting an active lifestyle. Regardless of age, interests, or skill levels, there are a number of activities for you to choose from. Be a sports enthusiast or a gaming wizard - any time of the year, we've got you covered. A fully equipped Cross Fit Arena is here to entertain you with everything it has to offer.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to help build and nurture a sports culture by providing professional-quality equipment and a standard international experience at an affordable price.
